System Guide

Why a System? Improvement requires structure. Track progress, upgrade skills, and see real-time growth like an EXP bar filling up.

Ranks: Define your own milestones for ranks E through S (e.g., Rank C Fitness = 50 push-ups). The app tracks your level progression towards these ranks.

XP: Assign XP values to tasks (Workout: +10 XP, Read 10 pages: +5 XP). Accumulate XP to level up.

Quests: Set daily and weekly tasks. Completing them drives your progress.

Grind Mode: Consistency is key! Try not to break the chain. Completing at least one daily quest increases your streak.

Warning: This will erase all your progress.
